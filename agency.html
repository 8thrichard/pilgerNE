Department of Motor Vehicle

The Nebraska Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) oversees driver licensing, vehicle registration, and motor vehicle records in the state. It ensures road safety by administering driving tests and issuing permits and licenses. For services, visit your nearest DMV office at:

Stanton County Court

The Stanton County Court in Nebraska handles legal matters such as civil cases, small claims, probate, and misdemeanor criminal cases. It ensures justice is served at the county level and provides court services to residents. For assistance, visit the court at:
